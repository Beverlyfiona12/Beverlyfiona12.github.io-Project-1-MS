FIFI CAFE

About us

Fifi cafe is an, innovative food & beverage outlet located in the heart of Nairobi- in Karen. This modern Kenyan bistro concept was inspired by the love of brewing authentic coffee . Fifi cafe represents simplicity, smooth and contemporary vibes and we aim to bring locally-sourced premium-grade coffee, teas, dranks and funky eats to the local and expat community at a not-so-extravagant rate. Also, our cyclical Kenyan-inspired menu and unique ambience is a must-try experience for those in search for a chilled environment to catch up with their family, friends or colleagues in the day-time, but also for the funky cats who wanna get up to no good when the sun goes down.
